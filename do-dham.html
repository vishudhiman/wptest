Dodham Tour Package.

Uttarakhand is one of the recently impressed youngest states of India, which is positioned in the lap of Himalayas. It is extremely well-known for its Char Dham Yatra, pilgrimage tour destinations i.e. Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamunotri.

Badrinath Shrine: Badrinath is a well-known holy shrine of Hindus, which is to be found in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand. It is measured as the most main sites of Char Dham pilgrimage and is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Positioned at an altitude of 3,415 meters above sea level, Badrinath temple is balanced on the banks of Alaknanda River. It is bounded by Nar and Narayan mountains and is located at a distance of 301 km from Rishikesh. It was established by Adu Shankaracharya in 9th century and attracts lakhs of pilgrims each year from distant places. More than a few re-built have been carries out in the temple building after its construction. June to September months are most excellent for visiting this Hindu shrine as the roads are blocked due to deep snowfall in the winter season.

Kedarnath Shrine : Kedarnath is one more holy pilgrimage shrine of Char Dham Yatra pilgrimage tour. It is positioned in Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, in the lap of Himalayas. Kedarnath is the furthest shrines among all the Char Dham shrines and is to be found at an altitude of 3,584 meters above sea level near to Mandakini River. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, Kedarnath shrines present an attractive sight of snow capped mountains and verdant green valleys. The shrine opens for pilgrimage in April end and closes in October end as it remains cut off from rest part of the world due to heavy snowfall in the Himalayan ranges. There are a number of attractions near to Kedarnath shrine like Ukhimath, Agastyamuni, Phauli- Pasalat Devi Maa, Kalimath, Bamsu and Trijugi Narayan.

Char Dham Yatra Package with Itinerary

Duration : 4 Nights / 5 Days

Destinations Covered : Dehradun - Gaurikund - Kedarnath - Joshimat - Badrinath - Srinagar - Haridwar.

Day 01 : Dehradun to Gaurikund.
After arriving at Delhi, you will be taken to Haridwar. On reaching Haridwar, you will check-in, in the hotel and rest. The evening can be utilized to visit Hari Ki Pauri and Ganga Arti. The dinner and overnight stay will be at the hotel.

Day 02 : Gaurikund to Kedarnath.
After Breakfast at Haridwar, we proceed to Guptakashi. En route, you can enjoy the magnificent view of the river Mandakini. In the evening, we reach Guptkashi and check in, in hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 03 : Gaurikund to Badrinath.
After breakfast, we move towards Gaurikund, with your packed lunch. After a 32 km journey, you further trek to reach the destination. On the way, we halt for lunch and regain the trek to reach Kedarnath by late afternoon. On arrival, we go to the hotel and freshen up. After this you can go for darshan and pooja at the Kedarnath Temple. You can also visit Adi Shankaracharya Samadhi, located at the back of the temple. After this, you return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 04 : Badrinath to Srinagar.
After breakfast at Kedarnath, we once again resume trekking towards Gaurikund. On arrival, we drive back to Guptkashi for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 05 : Srinagar to Dehradun or Haridwar.
After breakfast, we leave for Srinagar. On arrival transfer the guests to Delhi Airport/Railway Station to board the flight/train for onward journey and tour terminates.
